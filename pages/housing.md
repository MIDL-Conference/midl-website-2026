# Housing

Participants will stay at either the Chientan Youth Activity Center (on-site) or The Tango Taipei JianTan (400m from the venue).

All participants will be provided with single occupancy in a double room, featuring either one double bed or two twin beds. Please note that both hotel locations and room configurations will be randomly assigned; specific requests for self-selection cannot be accommodated.

Below are photos of [Chientan Youth Activity Center](https://rwd.ezhotel.cloud/cyh/2) and [Tango Taipei JianTan](https://jt.tango-hotels.com/_en/01about/about.php). Swipe horizontally to explore each venue.
